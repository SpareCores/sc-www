Conference season 2026 is very much here, and we have a full calendar of events coming up. In mid-April, we spent three days at a [hackathon with OVHcloud and Crayon/SoftwareOne](/article/ovh-softwareone-hackathon-2026) in Paris, and next we are presenting and sponsoring at five conferences over the next few months, spread across Budapest (Hungary), London (United Kingdom), Warsaw (Poland), Houston (Texas, USA), and Falkenstein (Germany). Hope to see you there!

## May 18-20: Budapest Data+AI Forum (Hungary)

I have been attending (and often also giving a talk) at the <a href="https://budapestdata.hu/en/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Budapest Data+AI Forum</a> for over 10 years now. This year we are taking it a step further by officially sponsoring the conference for the first time. It is a three-day hybrid event that happens both in Budapest and in the cloud -- both of which our team calls home 😊

## June 5-7: PyData London 2026 (United Kingdom)

We will be presenting a talk titled `SELECT instance FROM cloud WHERE workload = ? ORDER BY cost_efficiency` at <a href="https://pydata.org/london2026" target="_blank" rel="noopener">PyData London 2026</a>. The <a href="https://pretalx.com/pydata-london-2026/talk/ABYV3J/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">full abstract is available on the conference site</a>. We are also sponsoring the conference and will have a booth on the main conference days with the team, so please come by to say hi, grab a hex sticker, or pick up a Metaflow tote bag (thanks to our friends at <a href="https://outerbounds.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Outerbounds</a>)! 🐍

## July 6-9: useR! 2026 in Warsaw (Poland)

We are also heading to <a href="https://user2026.r-project.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">useR! 2026</a> in Warsaw, where I will present a similar talk with a stronger R focus: `cloud::servers[workload == R][order(cost_efficiency)][1, eval(task)]`, building on our work presented at [useR! 2025](/article/upcoming-conferences-2025-Q3) and the [Resource Tracker](/article/resource-tracker-for-r) project. We are sponsoring this event as well, so look out for the Spare Cores booth!

I'll also extend my stay for the <a href="https://contributor.r-project.org/events/r-dev-days/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">R Dev Day</a> on July 10, which is a full day workshop with R Core members and contributors from all over the world -- obviously focused on R. I'm supporting the Weblate server for the translation teams. 🌏

## Sep 14-16: posit::conf(2026) in Houston, TX (USA)

I last attended rstudio::conf(2020), so I am super excited to be back for the rebranded <a href="https://conf.posit.co/2026/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">posit::conf(2026)</a>! A lot has changed in six years on both sides -- looking forward to reconnecting with the community. We will present a lightning talk about how Spare Cores can help DS/ML/AI teams optimize their cloud compute resource allocations. 🚀

## Sep 17: Hetzner Summit in Falkenstein (Germany)

We also received an invitation to present at the <a href="https://www.hetzner.com/hetzner-summit" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Hetzner Summit</a>, where we will share our cloud server benchmarking methodology and results. Honestly, doing a talk at a cloud provider's own event -- and right at the data center -- is a first for us and we are very excited about it! 🤖
