Spare Cores is the successor to cloudperf, an AWS-focused open dataset and toolset for exploring spot prices and stress-ng benchmarks, which began in 2017. This next-generation project was launched in Q3 of 2023 and gained momentum in 2024, particularly due to the support from NGI Search funding.
