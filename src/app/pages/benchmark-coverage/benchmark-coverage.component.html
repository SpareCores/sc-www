Benchmark Coverage

Spare Cores continuously inspects cloud servers and runs standardized benchmark workloads to measure performance and cost efficiency. Some instances cannot currently be inspected or benchmarked. This may happen when servers are temporarily unavailable (e.g. out of stock), not yet generally available (e.g. no pricing information available), restricted by vendor or account quotas (e.g. we cannot access larger GPU boxes on cloud credits), or affected by technical limitations such as API rate limits.
